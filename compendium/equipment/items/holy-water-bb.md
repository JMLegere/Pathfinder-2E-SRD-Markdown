# Holy Water *Item 1*

[consumable](rules/traits/consumable.md) [divine](rules/traits/divine.md) [good](rules/traits/good.md) [splash](rules/traits/splash.md)  

- **Price** 3 gp
- **Usage** held in 1 hand
- **Category** Consumable

This vial contains magical water blessed by a good deity. You can throw it as a ranged [Strike](rules/actions/strike.md). *Holy water* is a simple thrown weapon with a range increment of 20 feet. It shatters on a hit, dealing `1d6+1` good damage to the target and 1 good damage to every other creature within 5 feet of the target. *Holy water* damages only undead.

*Source: Beginner Box p. 53*
